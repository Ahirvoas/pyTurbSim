tuple pyts::profModels::jet.main::spd_coefs [static]

These are the 'Chebyshef' coefficients, copied from the Modules.f90 file of TurbSim v1.x.

The coefficients are: Column Definition 1 Jet maximum wind speed coefficient 2 Turbine Richardson number coefficient 3 uStar over the rotor coefficient 4 constant The rows are each of the 0-10th Chebyshev basis functions.
