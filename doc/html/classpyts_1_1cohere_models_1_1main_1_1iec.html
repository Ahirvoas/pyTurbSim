Detailed Description

The 'IEC' coherence model for the u velocity component between two points (z_i,y_i) and (z_j,y_j) is:

Coh=exp(-a*((f*r/uhub)^2+(0.12*r/Lc)^2)^0.5)

The IEC coherence is zero for the v and w components (coherence matrix is the identity matrix so that auto-spectra are retained).

Where: f is frequency. r is the distance between the two points. uhub is the hub-height mean velocity. a and Lc are constants according to, If IECedition<=2: a = 8.8 Lc = 2.45*min(30m,HubHt) If IECedition>=3: a = 12 Lc = 5.67*min(60m,HubHt)
